## ğŸ•¹ï¸ How to Play

1. **Objective**: The goal is to close more squares than your opponent.
2. **Taking Turns**: Players take turns clicking on the lines connecting adjacent dots.
3. **Scoring**: If a player completes the fourth side of a box, they color that box and score a point.
4. **Bonus Turn**: Completing a box grants the player an **extra turn**.
5. **Winning**: The game ends when all boxes are completed. The player with the most boxes wins!
